Welcome to M.Stevens (Attorny At law)

The firm is based in Cape Town CBD and established by Marcello Stevens on the 7th of November 2008 as a sole proprietor. The firm specializes in Criminal Litigation exclusively as the proprietor has vast experience in Criminal Litigation drawn from seven years in the service of the National Prosecuting Authority as a prosecutor. The firm deals with various complex criminal matters on District Court and Regional court levels and defended charges ranging from theft , rape, armed robbery, murder and dealing in narcotics.

Areas of Specialization

M.Stevens Attorneys brings extensive legal experience, with a strong track record in defending serious criminal matters including narcotics, violent crimes, and commercial offences. The proprietor is admitted to practice in the High Court and commenced accepting matters with High Court jurisdiction. Mr Stevens also assists new companies in forensic investigation drafting charges and guiding Directors in matters of fraud and disputed documents research.
